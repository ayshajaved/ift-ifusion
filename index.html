Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our products and services

What are the advantages of micro-inverters?

Micro-inverters offer module-level MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking), which means each solar panel operates independently. This results in higher energy harvest, especially in partial shading conditions. They also provide panel-level monitoring, enhanced safety, and easier system expansion.

How do I maintain my solar energy system?

Our solar systems require minimal maintenance. Regular cleaning of solar panels to remove dust and debris, occasional inspection of wiring and connections, and monitoring system performance through our app are typically all that's needed. We offer maintenance packages for commercial installations.

What certifications do your products have?

Our products are certified by international standards including TUV, INMETRO, EU CE, and EMC. These certifications ensure that our products meet global quality, safety, and performance standards.

How can I get a quote for my home or business?

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form, calling our office, or sending us an email. Our team will assess your energy needs and provide a customized solution with detailed pricing.

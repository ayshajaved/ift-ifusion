About Us

Learn about our mission, values, and expertise in inverter manufacturing

Our Company

Leading inverter manufacturing with global expertise

IFT iFusion is a subsidiary expansion of Nanjing Ifusion Technology Co., Ltd., specializing in advanced inverter manufacturing and power electronics for global markets. As a forward-thinking company, we are committed to delivering cutting-edge inverter solutions with superior performance and reliability.

Our parent company has established a strong reputation in power electronics manufacturing, and we bring that expertise and quality to serve industrial and commercial applications worldwide. With a focus on industrial inverters, grid-tie systems, and power management solutions, we provide comprehensive inverter technology for diverse market needs.

At IFT iFusion, we leverage advanced manufacturing capabilities and rigorous quality standards to produce inverters that meet international certifications and exceed customer expectations.

Our Philosophy

The principles that guide our business

Customer-Oriented

We place our customers at the center of everything we do, ensuring that our solutions address their specific needs and challenges. Our commitment to customer satisfaction drives our innovation and service excellence.

Integration and Innovation

We believe in the power of integrated solutions and continuous innovation. By combining cutting-edge technologies with practical applications, we create products that are both advanced and user-friendly.

Creating Value

Our ultimate goal is to create value for our customers, partners, and the broader community. Through sustainable energy solutions, we contribute to economic growth, environmental protection, and improved quality of life.

Our Certifications

Meeting international quality and safety standards

At IFT iFusion, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety. Our products undergo rigorous testing and certification processes to ensure they meet or exceed international standards.

Our certifications include:

  • TUV Certification
  • INMETRO Certification
  • EU CE Certification
  • EMC Certification

These certifications reflect our commitment to providing safe, reliable, and high-performance products to our customers. They also enable our products to be used in projects that require compliance with specific international standards.

Our Patents and Innovations

Pioneering advancements in solar technology

Innovation is at the heart of our business. Through our parent company, we have access to numerous patents and proprietary technologies that give our products a competitive edge in the market.

Our research and development team continuously works on improving existing products and developing new solutions to address emerging challenges in the renewable energy sector.

Some of our key innovations include:

  • Advanced module-level MPPT technology for micro-inverters

  • Enhanced energy storage solutions for residential applications
  • Smart monitoring systems for real-time performance tracking
